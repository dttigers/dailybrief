## What Was Built

**Brand token system:** 16 Vigil brand tokens defined in the Tailwind v4 `@theme` block within `vigil-pwa/src/index.css`:
- 5 Vigil Teal tokens (teal-50 through teal-800)
- 4 Vigil Warm Neutral gray tokens + surface
- 6 Status Accent tokens (info, warning, success — 50 and 400 variants each)
- 1 font-sans token pointing to Inter

**Inter font loading:** Loaded from Google Fonts CDN via `@import url(...)` in index.css plus `<link rel="preconnect">` tags in index.html for DNS prefetch performance.

**Theme color unification:** Both HTML `<meta name="theme-color">` and VitePWA manifest `theme_color` updated from `#0f172a` (old slate-900) to `#0F6E56` (Vigil Teal 600).
