- **Files modified:** `Scripts/install.sh`
- **Verification:** bird provisioned container directory at `~/Library/Mobile Documents/iCloud~com~jamesonmorrill~dailybriefmonitor/`; NSLog confirms non-nil URL
- **Committed in:** `10e4fd9`
