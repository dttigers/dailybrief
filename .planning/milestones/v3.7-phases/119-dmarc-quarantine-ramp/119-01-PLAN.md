| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Cloudflare DNS edit → public DNS resolvers | TXT record change is publicly visible the moment Cloudflare propagates; recipient mail servers (Gmail, etc.) consume `_dmarc.vigilhub.io` and apply policy on next send. Mistakes are visible to the entire internet, not just Vigil. |
