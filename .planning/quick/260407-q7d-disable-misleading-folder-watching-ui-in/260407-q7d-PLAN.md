       Form {

                   .foregroundStyle(.orange)
                   .padding(.vertical, 4)

4. **Preservation checks — DO NOT TOUCH:**
   - Do NOT modify SettingsViewModel.swift (zero diff)

Why `.disabled(true)` at Form level instead of per-control: SwiftUI's `.disabled` modifier propagates down the view hierarchy. Per-control disables would be noisier and easier to forget on a new control.
